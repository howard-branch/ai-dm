# World Lore — Innistrad in Decline

Innistrad is a land of dim ward-light, haunted roads, and isolated human
settlements that endure between forests, mountains, swamps, and vampire-held
estates. The disappearance of the archangel Avacyn has weakened the holy
protections that once held the worst horrors at bay.

## The immediate crisis

The Church of Avacyn secretly entrusted a strange tuning-fork relic — the
**Strionic Resonator** — to the Stensian prodigy **Katarina Brunn**, hoping she
could discover how to use it to recover Avacyn without breaking the Helvault.
Before she could finish her work, vampires struck Shadowgrange, killed Mayor
Ralph Brunn, abducted Katarina, and shattered what remained of the village's
confidence.

## The undead war

Katarina's fiancé **Siegfried Reinholdt**, an alchemist already flirting with
necro-alchemy, fled north in grief and rage. In Mauer Estate he turned fully
toward stitched abominations, zombie armies, and revenge. With the aid of the
young necromancer **Rinelda Smitt**, he now sends undead south through the
farbog and ruined settlements toward Shadowgrange.

## Themes

- Gothic isolation
- Failing faith and weakening wards
- Revenge curdling into monstrosity
- Cowardice, guilt, and communal shame
- Small acts of courage against overwhelming darkness
