# Chapter 5 — The Perfect Storm

Mauer Estate is the culmination of the adventure's themes: grief made tangible,
science made monstrous, and revenge elevated into ritual. The party can enter
by stealth, force, or bargain, but eventually they must confront Siegfried in the
storm tower before his ultimate creation comes to life.

## Core goals

- Gain entry to Mauer Estate
- Learn the full extent of Siegfried's obsession
- Stop the storm-powered creation in the laboratory tower
- Decide what mercy, if any, Siegfried deserves
