# Chapter 1 — Into Stensia

The characters enter Stensia by way of Hofsaddel Pass and descend into a land
that immediately announces its danger: ruined chapels, demon-cult caves,
haunted bridges, failing roadside wards, and travelers already half-lost to the
night. The chapter establishes the adventure's tone and points the party toward
Shadowgrange.

## Core goals

- Reach Shadowgrange alive
- Learn that Stensia is collapsing under supernatural pressure
- Meet at least one human face of the setting, such as Anton Wepper
- Gather clues tying cult activity, vampires, and the road north together
