# Chapter 4 — Into the Farbog

Once the immediate threat at Shadowgrange is addressed, the party must head
north through the farbog. The swamp is slow, wet, and psychologically hostile:
old ruins sink into the muck, hallucinations pass for monsters, and corpse
wagons mark the road back to Siegfried's estate.

## Core goals

- Follow the horde's route back north
- Survive the farbog's supernatural hazards
- Gather reagents, clues, and allies for the final chapter
- Reach the grove around Mauer Estate
