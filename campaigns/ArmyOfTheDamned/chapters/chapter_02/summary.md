# Chapter 2 — Shadowgrange

Shadowgrange is frightened, crowded, and ashamed. The characters arrive to a
community living on routine and denial, even as supernatural threats gather at
its doorstep. They can make allies, uncover cult activity, recover a relic from a
haunted manor, and piece together what happened to Katarina and Siegfried.

## Core goals

- Gain the trust of Shadowgrange's leaders
- Retrieve the Strionic Resonator from Brunn Manor
- Uncover signs of Skirsdag infiltration
- Learn that Siegfried fled north toward Mauer Estate
