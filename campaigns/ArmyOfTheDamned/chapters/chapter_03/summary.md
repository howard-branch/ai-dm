# Chapter 3 — Night of the Dead

The adventure pivots from investigation to crisis. Refugees from Mordov bring
word that an undead army marches south. The party must help Shadowgrange
choose between evacuation and defense, then carry that choice through as the
horde closes around the village.

## Core goals

- Hear the refugees' warning
- Influence the Shadowgrange council
- Survive the undead assault or evacuation effort
- Confirm that Siegfried and Rinelda are behind the horde
