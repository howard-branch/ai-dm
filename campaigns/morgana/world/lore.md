# World Lore — The Salt Coast
Two centuries after the **Naming Wars**, the Sword Coast has settled
into an uneasy peace. The Wars ended when the kings of the eight free
cities agreed never again to *speak the true name* of any living
sovereign in war or judgement — a custom enforced by the **Avowed of
Candlekeep**, who keep the secret indices.
A true name, spoken aloud and *meant*, can compel a soul. The dead
remember theirs. The living, mostly, do not.
## Powers in play
- **The Avowed of Candlekeep** — neutral library-order. Catalogue
  everything, judge nothing, share almost nothing.
- **The Tomeguard** — Candlekeep's standing watch. Plain folk, plain
  faith, deep suspicion of magic.
- **The Academy at Velmis** — Morgana's former home. Casts out students
  who breach the Naming Accord.
- **The Hollow Choir** — heretical cult who believe the dead should be
  *re-named*, not laid to rest. Suspected of seeding sarcophagi like
  the one in the Candlekeep courtyard.
## What Morgana knows
- Her exile is sealed: she cannot return to Velmis.
- Candlekeep keeps the only complete index of her family's true names —
  the **Vault of Veiled Names**.
- Something is moving in the Vault. Ravens have stopped roosting above it.
