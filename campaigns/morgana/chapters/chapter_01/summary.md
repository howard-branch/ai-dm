# Chapter 1 — The Gift of Knowledge
Morgana, an exiled witch‑necromancer, arrives at **Candlekeep** at the
end of a long pilgrimage. She carries a single, leather‑wrapped tome —
the *Gift* she means to trade for entry into the great library, and a
reading of the one shelf no acolyte may touch: the **Vault of Veiled
Names**.
The Avowed have grown nervous. Three nights ago a sarcophagus
unearthed beneath the courtyard cracked open of its own accord. Old
Beren, the gatekeeper, blames the wind. The apprentice raven‑keeper
**Ily** does not. The **Tomeguard** are arguing in the courtyard about
who saw what — and whether the dead thing inside is still inside.
Morgana feels the pull immediately: something in that sarcophagus
remembers her name.
## Chapter goals
- Gain admittance to Candlekeep (offer a `Gift`, intimidation, or stealth).
- Investigate the cracked sarcophagus in the courtyard.
- Decide what to do about the **Hollow Echo** — the revenant inside.
- Open the thread *Vault of Veiled Names* (carries into Chapter 2).
## Tone
Hushed, candle‑lit, conspiratorial. Long shadows; old vellum; ravens.
NPCs are wary of magic but desperate for answers.
