# Candlekeep
A six-sided fortress-library on the cliffs above the Sword Coast. Walls
of pale, salt-bitten limestone enclose a warren of cloisters, towers,
and shelving stacks said to descend further beneath the rock than they
rise above it.
Visitors are admitted only on payment of a **Gift of Knowledge**: a
book, scroll, map, or recorded memory the keep does not yet possess.
The Avowed catalogue every gift; the **Keeper of Tomes** approves or
rejects each one.
## Key spaces (for Chapter 1)
- **Outer Gate** — manned by the Tomeguard. Heraldry: an open book on grey.
- **Courtyard** — cloistered square with a dry well at its center; the
  recently disturbed flagstones lie beside the east wall. *(Scene:
  `candlekeep_courtyard`.)*
- **Scriptorium** — long candle-lit hall of slanted desks, locked at
  dusk. Index cards live here. *(Scene: `scriptorium_after_dark`.)*
- **Rookery** — round tower above the courtyard. Ily lives here.
- **Vault of Veiled Names** — sub-basement; mentioned only in whispers.
## Mood
Wax, vellum, sea-wind, and the constant soft creak of bound spines
expanding and contracting in the salt air.
